Agentic BI is reshaping how organizations move from raw data to business decisions by combining autonomous AI agents with traditional business intelligence workflows. This practical guide covers what Agentic BI means for BI teams and business users, how it differs from conventional dashboards, and how Databricks Genie powers natural-language data exploration.
